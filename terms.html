Welcome to Arya Boys PG. These terms and conditions outline the rules and regulations for the use of our accommodation services and website. By booking with us, you accept these terms in full.

1. Booking & Reservation Policy

1.1 Booking Process

  • Online Booking: Reservations can be made through our website or direct contact
  • Booking Confirmation: All bookings are subject to availability and confirmation
  • Required Information: Valid ID proof, contact details, and emergency contact required
  • Age Requirement: Minimum age of 18 years required for booking
  • Verification: All bookings are subject to verification and approval

1.2 Payment Terms

  • Security Deposit: One month's rent as refundable security deposit
  • Advance Payment: First month's rent to be paid at the time of booking
  • Monthly Rent: Due on or before the 5th of each month
  • Payment Methods: Cash, UPI, Bank Transfer, Online Payment accepted
  • Late Payment: ₹100 per day penalty for payments after 5th of the month

Note: All payments are non-transferable between different months or residents.

2. Check-in & Check-out Procedures

2.1 Check-in Requirements

  • Valid ID Proof: Aadhar Card, Passport, Driving License, or College ID
  • Medical Certificate: Health certificate from registered doctor (if required)
  • Guardian Contact: Valid emergency contact details of parent/guardian
  • Check-in Time: Between 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM
  • Room Inspection: Mandatory room condition verification before occupancy

2.2 Check-out Procedures

  • Notice Period: 30 days advance notice required for check-out
  • Room Handover: Room to be handed over in original condition
  • Damage Assessment: Any damages will be deducted from security deposit
  • Final Settlement: All dues to be cleared before check-out
  • Refund Process: Security deposit refunded within 7-10 working days

3.1 Prohibited Activities

  • Alcohol & Drugs: Strictly prohibited in premises and rooms
  • Smoking: Not allowed anywhere in the building
  • Illegal Activities: Any unlawful activities will result in immediate termination
  • Noise Disturbance: Loud music or noise after 10:00 PM not allowed
  • Unauthorized Guests: Overnight guests without prior permission prohibited
  • Subletting: Sharing or subletting rooms to others strictly forbidden

3.2 Expected Behavior

  • Mutual Respect: Maintain courteous behavior with other residents and staff
  • Cleanliness: Keep personal and common areas clean and hygienic
  • Property Care: Handle all furniture and equipment with care
  • Reporting Issues: Immediately report any maintenance or security issues
  • Visitor Guidelines: All visitors must be registered at reception

Warning: Violation of conduct rules may result in immediate termination without refund of deposits.

4.1 Internet & WiFi

  • Fair Usage: WiFi provided for educational and general use
  • Prohibited Content: Illegal downloads, streaming of inappropriate content forbidden
  • Speed Limitations: Heavy downloads may be subject to speed restrictions
  • Device Limit: Maximum 3 devices per resident

  • Special Diets: Special dietary requirements subject to additional charges
  • Outside Food: Outside food allowed in rooms, not in common dining area

  • Washing Machines: Available on first-come, first-served basis
  • Room Cleaning: Daily cleaning service provided
  • Linen Change: Bed sheets and pillow covers changed weekly
  • Personal Laundry: Residents responsible for personal clothing care

5.1 Security Measures

  • Entry/Exit Logs: All movements recorded for security
  • Visitor Policy: All visitors must provide valid ID and register
  • Key Management: Room keys/cards are non-transferable
  • Night Security: Security guard available from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM

5.2 Emergency Procedures

  • Fire Safety: Fire extinguishers and exits clearly marked
  • Medical Emergency: First aid available, nearby hospital contacts provided
  • Emergency Contacts: 24/7 emergency numbers displayed prominently
  • Evacuation Plan: Emergency evacuation procedures posted in each room

6.1 Cancellation Terms

  • Early Cancellation: Cancellation 7+ days before check-in: Full refund minus processing fee
  • Late Cancellation: Cancellation within 7 days: 50% refund of advance payment
  • No-Show: Failure to check-in without notice: No refund
  • Mid-Term Exit: No refund for partial month stays

6.2 Refund Processing

  • Processing Time: 7-10 working days for refund processing
  • Refund Method: Refunds processed through original payment method
  • Deductions: Damages, outstanding dues deducted from refunds
  • Documentation: Proper documentation required for all refund requests
