Ranking Factors for Galgotias PGs

We ranked the PGs in the Galgotias region based on five main parameters:

  • Commute Ease: Auto availability and travel time to university gates.
  • Amenities: Availability of WiFi, laundry, RO water, and attached bathrooms.
  • Safety: CCTV surveillance and security staff.
