7. 10 Tips to Save Money as a Student in Greater Noida

  • Choose an all-inclusive PG — saves ₹1,500–₹3,000/month on utilities and meals vs. separate arrangements
  • Live near your campus — ₹10 auto vs ₹50-80/day in commute saves ₹1,000-₹2,000/month
  • Share OTT subscriptions — split Netflix/Hotstar with roommates (₹40/month each)
  • Cook on weekends — make snacks/meals on holidays instead of ordering Zomato
  • Use college canteen — subsidized meals at ₹30-50 when PG mess is closed
  • Carpool to college — share autos with 3-4 students to cut per-person costs
  • Use student discounts — Google for student offers on Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra
  • Buy secondhand textbooks — seniors often sell books at 50-70% discount
  • Avoid impulse spending — set a weekly entertainment budget and stick to it
  • Track expenses in an app — Walnut, Splitwise, or Excel to monitor spending
